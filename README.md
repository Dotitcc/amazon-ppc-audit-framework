# 📊 Comprehensive Amazon PPC Audit Framework
> **Created by:** Abdullah Al Masud | [@MasudLabs](https://github.com/MasudLabs)
> **Purpose:** A standardized, step-by-step operating procedure (SOP) to audit existing Amazon PPC accounts, identify wasted ad spend, and uncover immediate scaling opportunities.
---

## 1. Macro Account Health (The 30,000 ft View)
Before diving into individual campaigns, establish the baseline health of the account over the last 30, 60, and 90 days.

* **Total Sales vs. PPC Sales:** What percentage of revenue is dependent on ads? (Target: < 40% depending on lifecycle).
* **ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sales):** Is the current ACoS profitable based on the product's break-even margin?
* **TACoS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales):** The true measure of account health. Is ad spend driving organic ranking?
* **Overall Conversion Rate (CVR):** Are the ads sending traffic to listings that actually convert? (Target: 10%+).

---

## 2. Campaign Architecture & Structure
A messy account is impossible to scale. Evaluate how the current campaigns are organized.

* **Naming Conventions:** Are campaigns named logically? (e.g., `SP | Exact | Main KW | Product X`).
* **Portfolio Utilization:** Are portfolios being used to control budget caps by brand, category, or product line?
* **Campaign Segmentation:** Are match types (Broad, Phrase, Exact) separated into different campaigns to control budgets?
* **Single Keyword Campaigns (SKWCs):** Are top-performing "Hero" keywords isolated into their own campaigns for maximum budget control?

---

## 3. Wasted Spend & "Bleeder" Analysis
The fastest way to improve ACoS is to stop spending money on things that don't work. Pull the last 60 days' **Search Term Report (STR)**.

* **High Spend, Zero Sales:** Identify all search terms with $X spend (e.g., $15-$20) and 0 orders. **Action: Add to Negative Exact.**
* **High Clicks, Zero Sales:** Identify terms with 10+ clicks and 0 orders. **Action: Add to Negative Exact.**
* **High ACoS Bleeders:** Identify terms converting well above the break-even margin. **Action: Reduce bids or pause if irrecoverable.**
* **ASIN Targeting Bleeders:** Check Sponsored Display and Product Targeting campaigns for competitor ASINs draining budget without converting.

---

## 4. Keyword & Search Term Harvesting
Are we capitalizing on what *is* working?

* **Auto Campaign Mining:** Are profitable search terms from Auto campaigns being harvested and moved to Manual Exact campaigns?
* **Negative Strategy in Auto:** When a keyword is moved to a Manual campaign, is it being negated (Negative Exact) in the Auto campaign to prevent internal competition? (Keyword Isolation).
* **Broad/Phrase Mining:** Are long-tail discovery terms being actively extracted and targeted directly?

---

## 5. Placements & Bidding Strategy
Evaluate how bids are being modified based on where the ads actually appear.

* **Top of Search (ToS) vs. Rest of Search:** Check the `Placements` tab. If ToS has a 20% CVR and Rest of Search has a 5% CVR, are ToS multipliers applied?
* **Dynamic Bidding:** Are campaigns set to *Up & Down*, *Down Only*, or *Fixed*? (Recommendation: Use *Down Only* or *Fixed* for new campaigns to gather clean data).
* **Budget Caps:** Are highly profitable campaigns running out of budget by 2 PM?

---

## 6. Ad Format Penetration
Is the account utilizing all available real estate on Amazon?

* **Sponsored Products (SP):** The foundation. Are both keyword and ASIN targeting utilized?
* **Sponsored Brands (SB):** Are custom images and video ads being used to capture top-of-funnel traffic?
* **Sponsored Display (SD):** Is retargeting set up to capture shoppers who visited the listing but didn't purchase?
* **Brand Defense:** Are we bidding on our own branded terms and targeting our own ASINs to keep competitors off our detail pages?

---

## 7. Retail Readiness (The Foundation)
PPC cannot fix a bad listing. A quick audit of the product detail page is mandatory.

* **Title & Images:** Is the main image click-worthy? Does the title contain the absolute main keyword?
* **Reviews & Ratings:** Is the product rated 4.0 stars or higher? (Running aggressive PPC on a 3-star product is a waste of capital).
* **A+ Content & Video:** Are these present to maximize the conversion rate of the expensive PPC traffic we are driving?

---

### 🚀 Next Steps & Action Plan
*Based on this audit, generate a priority checklist for the client:*

1. **Immediate (Days 1-3):** Cut wasted spend, add negative keywords, adjust bids on high-ACoS targets.
2. **Short-Term (Days 4-14):** Restructure naming conventions, isolate top-performing keywords, implement Top of Search multipliers.
3. **Long-Term (Days 15-30+):** Launch Sponsored Video, expand into broad discovery campaigns, set up ASIN defense.
